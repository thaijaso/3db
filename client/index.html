Our Philosophy

Here at The 3 Dream Builders, Inc. we strongly believe that customer service is the foundation for a successful business. We are in business because we love building people's dreams into reality. We take pride in been a local Portland/Beaverton contractor specializing in two major trades - Stone and Paint. Repeat and referred customers account for over 40% of our current business. You can count on us to provide the highest quality service to you and your family, at a competitive price.

We consistently adhere to professional standards, comply with safety regulations and remain convinced that top-of-the-line products and workmanship deliver the best long-term results and customer relationships. Every one of our valued customers can expect a knowledgeable and friendly project manager and team, who will strive to complete your project in a professional, timely manner.

We have successful business partnerships with many reliable, registered and insured contracting companies to assist you with all aspects of your maintenance, repair or renovation projects. Professional decorating services are also available. Whether you need remodeling, demolition or new construction for commercial, office or a historical home, The 3 Dream Builders, Inc. has the qualified personnel for each trade. We pay attention to every little detail to transform your project from ordinary to extraordinary.

Let us know how we can serve you!
